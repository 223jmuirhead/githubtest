Gameplay

Portal features very unique gameplay because of the use of portals and the portal gun. Since this concept will be new to most players the devolpers of the game made the first levels of the game show the different mechanics of portals without out right explaining it them. This choice helps make the gameplay flow more organically comparred to just explaining how they work. A great example of this is shown in the first level of the game. The game starts with you in a room with glass walls and no aparent exit. The only objects in the room are a futuristic bed, a radio playing a catchy tune, and a toilet. Looking through the glass the player can see a timer on the wall counting down from one minuet, at the end of this timer a portal spawns letting the player out of the room. This is a deliberate choice which gives you time to take in your surroundings. The devolper comentary explains how play testers thought portals took you into different locations or entire differnt dimentions when in reality they just take you to a different spot in the room. The minuet spent in the starting room combined with the glass walls makes it so when the player can see themself exit the room and once they are outside they recognize the room they were in. This is just one of the many ways Portal will subtly teach you new mechanics of the game.
