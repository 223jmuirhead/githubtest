Portal 2

After the large success of Portal it only made sence for Valve to make a sequal. In 2011, just 4 years after the release of Portal came Portal 2. Portal 2 takes everything great about Portal and builds on it further. The graphics were updated, the game is longer, and theres more mechanics which allow for more complex puzzles. Where the orginal Portal leaned more into a creepy atmosphere with the occasional joke, Portal 2 seems to have a bigger focus on the comedy which it does greatly. Portal 2 also introduced two new modes which greatly increase the quality of the game. The first of these being a multiplayer campain. You both get a portal gun and have to work together to solve puzzles. Secondly there is a level creator, this allows you to make, share, and play your own custom test chambers which greatly increases the replayibility of the game.
