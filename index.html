Portal


Portal is a 3D puzzle platformer released in 2007 by Valve. Portal was part of Valve's "The Orange Box" game bundle along side with Team Fortress 2 and Half-Life 2. In the game you take control of Chell as you solve puzzles to make your way through the Aperture Science Enrichment Center. Using the portal gun you are able create a blue and orange portal that allows you to travel or bring objects between them. Your only company while going through these test chambers is GLaDOS, an AI made by the same company testing you, Aperature Science. GLaDOS adds an element of humor to the game, jeering at the player for making mistakes or making empty promises of the test chambers being safe. At least she promises cake if you finish all the test chambers.

In 2005 a small free indie game called Narbacular Drop was released with the game's main gimmick also being portals. Upon seeing the game Valve offered to hire the team that made it, they accepted and would go on to make Portal.
